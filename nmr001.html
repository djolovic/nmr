In may 1965,a Radio Station in Finland , managed to obtain an interview with The Beatles for a Radio Programme, Kaleidoscope. As far as we are aware, it was never broadcast.

The interviews took place at the Twickenham Film Studios, and was carried out by Hilkka Kantelinen, who had worked in England as an au pair in the early 60's.

When she returned to Finland, she started working as a journalist and interviewed a host of celebrities for both Radio and Iskelma, a popular newspaper.

Hilkka also spoke to Richard Lester, the Director of both A Hard Days Night and Help! and Betty Glasgow who was responsible for make up and hair styles in the movie.

We don't want to give too much away, but in these interviews, you will hear Betty talk about the letters she receives from around the world about the Beatlecut and John describes the lengths he has to go to in order to escape from the fans.
