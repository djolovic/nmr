Once upon a long ago or maybe twice in the Land Beneath The Sea from John O' Groats to....Radio City the 4th of April 2009...

An historic occasion in more ways than one. A live reunion of the two remaining Icons from the most Iconic band of all time..the Beatles....and a rendition of Cosmically Conscious, a tune written in India.

In 1994, we had the "reunion" of the Threetles as they were affectionately named, now it's just the Tootles.

All to promote Transcandental Meditation some 40 years on....Luminaries queued up to join Ringo and Paul on Stage...Mike Love, David Lynch, Sheryl Crow, Eddie Vader, Ben Harper, Moby and last but by no means least Donovan, who was in India with the Beatles....such a shame he didn't sing the extra verse to Hurdy Gurdy Man...

What else is there to say except that we never expected to hear those immortal words...
